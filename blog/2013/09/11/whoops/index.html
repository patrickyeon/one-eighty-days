Whoops

Sep 11th, 2013

Wherein the ball is thoroughly dropped.

That was a disappointing little experiment. Only three releases done, and then total radio silence for a month and a half. I thought that I was likely to make it out to about 10 days before I ran out of stuff ready to fire off, but that was horribly optimistic. It doesn’t help that as soon as I missed a day, I was ready to really let things slip, and eventually nearly forget about it.

I’m still drawn to the idea of forcing myself to publish, even though I’m not at all up to the challenge of 180 days straight of pushing out new content. Not yet, at least. I’m going to try coming at it from the other end, starting off with a release once a week. When that level of effort is my new normal, I’ll step it up and make myself uncomfortable trying for more frequent releases.

My goal is still the same as when I started: 180 releases, 180 days. I’m just accepting that there’s a longer road to take to get there than I was hoping for at first. I think the underlying drive is still the same; I want to push myself to be more productive than I currently am. I feel like I have a large amount of potential that I am squandering.
